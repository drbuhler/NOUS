The Context

Meeting the Moment

As converts pour into the Orthodox Church, the need for stable foundations has never been greater. We need more than just full pews; We need faithfulness. That starts with stable homes, a robust clergy, and a new generation of Orthodox teachers to help educate our children.

Currently, many Orthodox parish schools, private schools, and homeschool co-ops exist:

  • Some are 20+ years old: their challenge may be transforming into a generational institution.
  • Some schools are 1-19 years old: their challenge may be maintaining momentum, motivation, enrollment, and capital stability.
  • Still other initiatives are brand new -- or yet to launch: their challenge is gathering the people, the place, and the finances under a single vision.

    • NOUS aims provides solutions to schools of any size, in each stage.

    We equip parishes, communities, and families with the tools they need to thrive.

    Scalable Models

    Build for Success at Any Size

    Institutions require different foundations based on their mission and scope.

    Small

    Homeschool Coop

    The Model: Parent-led communities meeting 1–3 days a week | 2-15 families | Focus on supplement and fellowship.

    Our Recommendation

    NOUS affiliates should tap the Homeschool Coop Resource Bank and receive Peer Support

    Medium

    Parish School

    The Model: 5-day schools and 3-day schools, often hosted by or connected to a parish) with a majority Orthodox student body and full administrative team | 10-100 families | Focus on professionalization, training, and annual fundraising.

    Our Recommendation

    NOUS members should go through the Herzog SchoolBox program, then tap Resource Bank & purchase consultant support

    Large

    Community School

    The Model: 5-day, full-service private schools in major metropolitan statistical areas. These schools have Orthodox leadership and many Orthodox families within a majority Non-Orthodox student body) serving the wider community with broad classical vision. | 50-250 families+ | Focus on institutional stability, generational success, and endowments.

    NOUS Recommendation

    Consider a St. Constantine School Partnership; and purchase consultant support
