Welcome! This blog is the news desk of **National Orthodox United Schools** — a place for updates from the network, essays on Orthodox classical education, and practical help for school leaders, homeschool parents, and teachers.

Here's what you can expect in the coming months:

- Updates on the **2026 St. Nektarios Orthodox Educators Conference** (November 6–7, online) — [reserve your spot](/conference/)
- Profiles of schools in the [NOUS directory](/#schools)
- Practical guidance from the [School in a Box](https://buhler.notion.site/St-Andrew-School-in-a-Box-f4868e0ee5b542c9b41b45ae803cea33) resource library

If you are putting your hand to the plough of Orthodox education — as a founder, board member, teacher, or parent — we would love to [hear from you](mailto:dionysiusbuhler@gmail.com).

Glory to Jesus Christ!
