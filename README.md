# OrthodoxSchools | NOUS | National Association of Orthodox Schools, United 

The official webpage of NOUS. 

We offer a one-stop full service suite of solutions for Orthodox Christian schools, coops, churches, and families. 

We also connect and curate the best resources out there to reduce overwhelm and get people moving forward. 

Our motto is "wisdom through repentance" -- only by humility and prayer and spiritual labor can we clean the heart and thus see God and the logoi in creation. 

Thank you for stopping by.
