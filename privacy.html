Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Introduction

The bottom line is that we will never use or sell your personal information to others. While this is enough, there is a growing trend of sites being clear about how they collect and use visitor information. This is a positive trend, in our view. So we add a bit more detail

Cookies and Log Files

Like nearly all websites, this site may store cookies on your computer. These collect information, but we have no direct access or control over them. To disable cookies, use your browser’s settings. We also use log files to help learn about when, from where, and how often traffic flows to this site (such as IP address, type of browser, number of clicks, etc.). None of this type of information is linked to anything that is personally identifiable.

Affiliate Links & Amazon

The NOUS network may occasionally participate in affiliate programs, such as the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. This means we may earn a very small commission on products (like educational books) you buy after entering through any of our pages.

These commissions never cost you any extra money, and we will never recommend a product just to make a sale. We only link to resources that we honestly respect and commend to your interest. These small commissions help support the network and allow us to continue providing resources to Orthodox educators.

Amazon uses cookies to help make sure we receive a commission when you buy a product after clicking on a link. You can read Amazon’s Privacy Policy for more details.

Google Analytics & Site Stats

Google Analytics is a web analytics tool we use to help understand how visitors engage with this website. It reports website trends using cookies and web beacons without identifying individual visitors. You can read the Google Analytics Privacy Policy, or use Google’s Chrome app to opt out of analytic data collection.

To measure performance, Google uses small strings of text (known as cookies) that are placed on your computer when you click on ads. Cookies typically remain active on your computer for about 30 days. You can read more about Google Site Stats on their platform.

Email Communications

Visitors who reach out to us regarding directory inclusion, consulting services, or conference registration do so via a secure email system. We reserve the right to contact those individuals with information directly related to the NOUS network and upcoming events. You may ask to be removed from our contact list at any time.
